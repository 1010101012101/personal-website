Work

ExxonMobil

Reliability Engineering Intern • Beaumont, TX• May 2016 - August 2016

Working as an intern at ExxonMobil's largest lubricant plant came with significant challenges and even greater development. I performed an effectiveness study on the finished oil product storage tank discharge systems, analyze pump curves and operational demands to upgrade gearbox ratios on six pumps, and initiated equipment strategy develpment for local pressure control equipment on tank discharge systems. This experience came with an extensive combination of technical and professional learning.

ExxonMobil

Engineering/Manufacturing Intern • Olathe, KS • May 2015 - August 2015

My first internship with ExxonMobil introduced me to working in a manufacturing facility. The plant manufactured commercial and industrial greases. During my time there, I designed two Nitrogen blanketing stations to increase raw material shelf life. I also compiled data and generated gantt chart models identifying bottlenecks within the manufacturing process of three grease products.

RMF Engineering

Mechanical Engineering Co-op • Durham, NC• August 2013 - May 2015

I had the opportunity to complete three co-op rotations, or twelve months total, on the Infrastructure team at RMF Engineering. My team designed steam and chilled water systems for large scale and critical applications. I specifically developed and edited AutoCAD drawings and Revit models for company projects, aided in the design process for three chilled water and steam distribution systems, and enhanced my professional communication skills by participating in the RMF chapter of Toast Masters.

CaptiveAire Systems

Research and Development Intern • Raleigh, NC • May 2013 - August 2013

My first internship gave me an initial taste of engineering in industry as a part of the CaptiveAire Research and Development team.
